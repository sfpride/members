Nominations for Board of Directors Election Have Begun

  • Nominations opened at the July Member meeting, and close at the August Member Meeting.
  • You must be a current member in good standing to make or second a nomination.
  • You may not nominate yourself.
  • Nominees must be members in good standing at the time of the election (they can join after being nominated).
  • Nominations may be submitted:
    1. verbally at both July and August Member meetings,
    2. sent in to bodnominations@sfpride.org, or
    3. mailed to the office (
      1663 Mission Street, SF, CA 94103
      ) postmarked by the closing date.
  • Please include contact information with your nomination.
