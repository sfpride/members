Become a Member Today!

The San Francisco Lesbian Gay Bisexual Transgender Pride Celebration Committee is an ever-evolving member-and community-based organization. Our membership assists the organization in setting the vision for the organization and the annual Celebration and Parade in addition to raising funds and conducting outreach for the organization. By becoming a member of San Francisco Pride you will have a direct voice in shaping an event that educates and gives back to the LGBT Community.

Benefits of membership include participation in the nomination and selection of Grand Marshals, choosing the yearly theme of the Celebration and Parade as well as voting on the selection of the Board of Directors. These are just some of the ways in which you can be fully engaged with Pride and the greater LGBT community no matter where you live! In addition to what has been mentioned above, all members of Pride receive the following General Member Benefits:

  • Invitations to special events throughout the year
  • Voting rights after 60-days of membership (this includes voting for candidates for the Board of Directors, the event theme, and Community Grand Marshals)
  • Your name published in our annual publication 'InsidePride,' and on our website

Membership Registration Platform Change

What we're doing
Essentially, we're moving everything to a new platform: Network for Good. On April 1, we're phasing out 123 Signup and using Network for Good as our membership management and donation platform.
What we need you to do
Right now, nothing! But when it comes time to renew your SF Pride membership, we will need to switch you over.
How do I renew my membership?
Whenever the end of your current membership approaches, you will receive an email notification from us directing you to renew it.
Joining and Renewal are the same process: click the link, choose an amount for dues, and select "donate".

Please Note: the platform we use to manage our membership dues is ordinarily used as a donation platform. We too use it to track charitable donations to our organization. Because of this, the terminology used on Network for Good is focussed around "donations".

Please contact us at membership@sfpride.org, and we will be more than happy to assist.
